Pancakes

Description

The contents of this page are AI slop generated just to complete this HTML project. We have a basic recipe for pancakes from Google's AI overview with a ChatGPT-generated image to kick things off.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt) in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the wet ingredients (milk, eggs, melted butter) separately.
  3. Combine the wet and dry mixtures gently.
  4. Pour a ladle of batter onto a hot, greased skillet and flip until golden brown.
