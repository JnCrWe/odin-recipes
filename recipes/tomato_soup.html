Tomato Soup

Description

The contents of this page are AI slop generated just to complete this HTML project. We have a basic recipe for tomato soup from Google's AI overview with a ChatGPT-generated image to kick things off.

Ingredients

  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Vegetable broth
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a pot and cook diced onions until soft.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Blend until smooth.
